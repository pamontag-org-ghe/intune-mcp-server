# Use Case 8 - Get Compliance Policies Assigned to Device

## Description

This use case describes the process of retrieving compliance policies assigned to a device in Intune. It outlines the steps involved in identifying and resolving compliance issues on devices.

## Question to answer

Which compliance policies are assigned to the device?

## APIs Endpoints

Get Compliance Policies Assigned to Device:

GET https://graph.microsoft.com/beta/deviceManagement/managedDevices/DEVICEID/deviceCompliancePolicyStates/
